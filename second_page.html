There are many endangered marine species outside the seahorses in Spain. The most endangered marine species in the world is the Vaquita. The main cause of the endangerment of Vanquitas is overfishing. They often die from being entangled in fishing nets. The fishermen do not even intend to capture the Vanquita; however, they often cast wide nets to catch a ton of fish at once. This type of fishing is dangerous as it can lead to endangered species also getting caught in the net. The reason that this is so dangerous to the Vanquita is that they have a small range of swimming, and the range that they inhabit is home to many valuable fish. They share the waters with the totoaba fish, which is extremely valued because the swim bladders fetch a high price in the black market as well as its medicinal properties in Chinese medicine. This is a common theme among endangered marine animals, and poor fishing practices lead to severe population decline across multiple species. It is important for these governments to shut down these illegal practices as soon as they start so we can look out for our ocean’s health. These animals keep these ecosystems’ populations in check and provide a balanced ocean living environment.

List of top endangered animals

  1. Vaquita
  2. North Atlantic Right Whale
  3. Yangtze Finless Porpoise
  4. Hawaiian Monk Seal
  5. Hawksbill Turtles and Kemp’s Ridley Turtles
